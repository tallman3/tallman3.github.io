Key factors

Below are the key factors to differentiate between funds

  • Yield - how much a return the fund provides per year
  • MER (Management Expense Ratio) - the annual fee charged by the fund
  • AUM (Assets Under Management) - the amount of assets the fund controls
  • STARS - The Morningstar rating for the fund
  • ALPHA - The value that managers add by active management of the fund. Higher is better.
  • Sharpe Ratio - The return compared to the amount of risk. Higher is better.
